## Commission Structure

| Plan | Price | Rate | Your Earning Per Customer |
|------|-------|------|------------------------|
| Pro Monthly | \$11.99/mo | 25% | \~\$3.00/month for 12 months (\~\$36 total) |
| Pro Annual | \$99/yr | 25% | \~\$24.75 one-time |
| Lifetime | \$349 | 30% | \~\$104.70 one-time |

<Note>
Monthly referrals earn you recurring commissions for 12 months. Annual and Lifetime referrals pay out once per customer.
</Note>

## Setting Up Payouts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Partner Dashboard">
    Navigate to your Partner Dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Set Up Payouts">
    Find the "Set Up Payouts" button in the Payout Setup section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe Onboarding">
    Follow the Stripe onboarding process (takes ~5 minutes)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Bank Information">
    Enter your bank account details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification Complete">
    Once verified, you'll see "Payouts Enabled" status
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can manage your bank details anytime by clicking "Manage Payouts" to access your Stripe Express Dashboard.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long do referral cookies last?" icon="cookie">
    30 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 30 days, you get credit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When do I get paid?" icon="calendar">
    Payouts are processed on the 1st of each month for balances of $50 or more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see who I've referred?" icon="users">
    Yes, your partner dashboard shows referral statistics, though individual user details are kept private.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if a referral cancels?" icon="user-minus">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple promo codes?" icon="tags">
    Currently, each partner receives one promo code. Contact us for special arrangements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how much I can earn?" icon="infinity">
    No cap on earnings. The more customers you refer, the more you earn.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
