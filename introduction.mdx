## What is Storehouses?

Storehouses is a privacy-first portfolio management platform designed for collectors and investors of alternative assets. Track and manage your precious metals, luxury watches, gems, jewelry, wine, spirits, fine art, arms & armor, and other valuable collectibles all in one secure place.

## Key Features

### Portfolio Management
- **Dashboard** - Visual overview with charts, allocation breakdowns, and portfolio statistics
- **Items** - Add, view, edit, and manage individual assets with rich metadata
- **Collections** - Organize items into custom collections for better organization
- **Locations** - Track physical storage locations for your valuables

### Advanced Capabilities
- **Price Alerts** - Set up notifications for precious metals and other asset price movements
- **Heir Access** - Emergency access system for designated beneficiaries
- **Export & Reporting** - Generate PDF reports or export data to CSV for insurance and tax purposes
- **Sharing** - Create secure, read-only links to share your portfolio with advisors or family
- **Wealth Garden** - Visualize your portfolio growth over time

## Subscription Plans

Storehouses offers flexible pricing to fit your needs:

- **Free Plan** - Track up to 50 items with core features and one default collection
- **Pro Plan** ($12/month or $99/year) - Unlimited items and collections with advanced features like alerts and heir access
- **Lifetime Plan** ($349 one-time) - One-time payment for lifetime access to all features
