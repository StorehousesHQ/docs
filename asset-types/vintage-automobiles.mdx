## Overview

Track your vintage automobile collection with detailed fields for make, model, year, VIN, and condition. Perfect for classic car collectors, automotive enthusiasts, and investors managing collector vehicles.

<Card title="Vintage Auto Features" icon="car">
  - Make, model, and year
  - VIN tracking
  - Condition assessment
  - Restoration documentation
  - Mileage and service history
  - Market value tracking
</Card>

## Adding a Vehicle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Add Item">
    Click "Add Item" from your dashboard or go to [storehouses.app/add](https://storehouses.app/add)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Vintage Automobiles">
    Choose "Vintage Automobiles" as the category
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in make, model, year, VIN, and condition
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Documentation">
    Upload photos and documentation (title, service records, appraisals)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save Item" to add to your collection
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Vehicle Fields

### Identification

<ParamField path="make" type="string">
  Vehicle manufacturer

  **Examples:**
  - Ferrari
  - Porsche
  - Ford
  - Chevrolet
  - Mercedes-Benz
  - Jaguar
  - Aston Martin
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="model" type="string">
  Specific model name

  **Examples:**
  - 250 GTO
  - 911 Carrera
  - Mustang Shelby GT500
  - Corvette Stingray
  - 300SL Gullwing
  - E-Type
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="year" type="number">
  Model year

  **Examples:**
  - 1963 (Split-window Corvette)
  - 1967 (Shelby GT500)
  - 1969 (Boss 429)
  - 1973 (Carrera RS)
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="vin" type="string">
  Vehicle Identification Number

  **Importance:**
  - Verifies authenticity
  - Tracks ownership history
  - Essential for insurance
  - Required for registration

  <Warning>
    Store VIN securely - it's sensitive information
  </Warning>
</ParamField>

### Condition

<ParamField path="condition" type="select">
  Overall vehicle condition

  **Standard classifications:**
  - **Concours** - Show-quality, flawless
  - **Excellent** - Near perfect, minimal wear
  - **Very Good** - Well maintained, minor imperfections
  - **Good** - Presentable, some wear
  - **Fair** - Running but needs work
  - **Project** - Requires significant restoration
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="mileage" type="string">
  Current odometer reading

  **Examples:**
  - "45,000 original miles"
  - "12,500 miles since restoration"
  - "Unknown - odometer replaced"
</ParamField>

### History

<ParamField path="provenance" type="textarea">
  Ownership and vehicle history

  **Include:**
  - Number of previous owners
  - Notable previous owners
  - Racing history
  - Accident history
  - Geographic history
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="restoration" type="textarea">
  Restoration details

  **Document:**
  - Restoration date and shop
  - Work performed
  - Parts replaced vs. original
  - Documentation and photos
  - Cost of restoration
</ParamField>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Document Thoroughly" icon="file-text">
    Maintain complete records:
    - Title and registration
    - Service history
    - Restoration receipts
    - Appraisals (annual update)
    - Photos (interior, exterior, engine, undercarriage)
    - Original documentation (window sticker, manuals)

    <Tip>
      Store documentation copies in Storehouses Secure Storage
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Store Properly" icon="warehouse">
    Protect your investment:
    - Climate-controlled storage
    - Battery tender when not in use
    - Proper car cover
    - Regular maintenance even when stored
    - Rodent prevention
    - Fuel stabilizer for long-term storage
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Insurance Considerations" icon="shield-check">
    Specialized coverage needed:
    - Agreed value coverage (not actual cash value)
    - Collector car insurance specialists
    - Document values with appraisals
    - Review coverage annually
    - Consider usage restrictions

    **Specialist insurers:**
    - Hagerty
    - Grundy
    - American Collectors
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Maintain Authenticity" icon="certificate">
    Preserve value:
    - Keep original parts when possible
    - Document any modifications
    - Matching numbers add value
    - Period-correct restorations preferred
    - Maintain original documentation
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I value my classic car?" icon="dollar-sign">
    **Valuation resources:**
    - Hagerty Valuation Tools
    - Sports Car Market
    - Bring a Trailer results
    - RM Sotheby's/Gooding auction results
    - Professional appraisals

    **Factors affecting value:**
    - Condition and originality
    - Matching numbers
    - Provenance
    - Rarity
    - Color combination
    - Documentation completeness
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Restored vs. original - which is worth more?" icon="wrench">
    **It depends on the car:**
    - **Survivor cars** with original paint/interior can command premiums
    - **Concours restorations** valuable for show cars
    - **Driver-quality** cars have strong market
    - **Matching numbers** always important

    <Note>
      Market preferences vary by marque and era
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often should I drive it?" icon="road">
    **General guidance:**
    - Regular use is good (prevents mechanical issues)
    - Avoid harsh conditions
    - Warm up properly before driving
    - Keep fuel fresh
    - Document mileage

    **Storage periods:**
    - Run engine periodically
    - Use battery tender
    - Rotate tires
    - Check fluids
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
